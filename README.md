## Reflections
One thing that went really well with our project was the completion of all our main features: Calendar, Tasks, Goals, and local storage capabilities. We also did really well at utilizing GitHub and ensuring that branches were created and managed using best practices. Our overall communication with our consistent team members also went well and allowed us to create a collaborative environment of brain storming and utilization. 

The main thing that did not go very well was the lack of our full team attending all class meetings. We can improve on team attendance by sending daily reminders of class and ensuring that our teammates commit to showing up.

We also ran into many issues when it came to the setup of our project, delaying our overall coding time. We could improve this by doing more research on the setup of Android Studio Code. This could also include a walk through with all team members.

We also could've done a better job of creating a step-by-step project that started small and could be built upon. Instead, we started broad and had to work our way to a smaller version of our project. This could be improved by creating a more detailed back log.

Overall, our project was a success and lead to many lessons being learned.
